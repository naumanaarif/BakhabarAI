### Advanced Reasoning & Multimodal Models

*   **`gemini-3.1-pro-preview`:** The latest reasoning-first model. Best for complex agentic workflows, deep problem-solving, and advanced coding. Features adaptive thinking capabilities and a 1M token context window.
*   **`gemini-3-flash-preview`:** Frontier-class intelligence built for speed. Tackles challenging multimodal problems (text, image, audio) faster and more efficiently than larger legacy models.
*   **`gemini-3.1-flash-lite`:** The ultimate high-volume workhorse. Delivers the quality of the Gemini 3 series but optimized for low-latency, cost-sensitive traffic at massive scale.
*   **`gemini-2.5-pro`:** A highly capable model for deep reasoning, coding, and complex agentic tasks.
*   **`gemini-2.5-flash`:** Balances high intelligence with speed. Ideal for low-latency tasks that still require strong reasoning capabilities.
*   **`gemini-2.5-flash-lite`:** Streamlined, ultra-efficient, and budget-friendly. Perfect for simple, high-frequency multimodal tasks where speed and cost are the top priorities.
